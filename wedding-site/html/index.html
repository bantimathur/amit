You are cordially invited to grace our wedding
with your presence on 07 February 2015

A fun-loving person by heart, Amit had an array of dreams & goals. A UI designer by profession, this creative person had achieved almost everything what people usually expect from a guy, but sadly this poor chap had long been in search of his Dreamgirl. And one fine day, there came a bliss in his life when he met Paridhi.

This young, beautiful, bubbly & confident girl belongs to Mumbai. A business analyst by profession, she was all there to win the world but fell in love with a stupid guy called Amit. Life was all about corporate job, before she met her ‘right match’. From there on, life was nothing less than a fairy tale, & all about bridal dresses, dreams and bliss.

Yeah, they say marriages are fixed in heaven. So was ours. But then it’s not easy to find your partner. I mean, it takes too long yaar! God should have atleast made a tattoo or provided some sort of hint to make our partner-hunt easy…lol. But as they say only time unfolds the right sequence of events. One day, our parents decided to make us meet. And it all began with a ‘Hi’ and we felt like being with each other for the rest of our lives. We really thank our parents for this!
Distance between Indore and Mumbai played the real villain. But we fought it hard with our love and made the time we spent together really special and memorable. Words are not sufficient to take you through our journey, so here is a glimpse of some moments we spent together.
